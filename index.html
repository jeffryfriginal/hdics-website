Who We Are

Built on Five Pillars

H

Honor & Glorify God

Everything we do flows from worship. God is first in our classrooms, in our halls, and in our community.

D

Dedication for Excellence

Academic rigor is not separate from faith — it is an expression of it. We pursue excellence as stewards of God-given gifts.

I

Integrity of Character

We form students whose word is their bond, whose actions match their convictions.

C

Compassionate Love

Love for God overflows into love for others. We build a community where every student is seen and cared for.

S

Stewardship of God

We teach students to hold all things lightly — time, gifts, resources — as faithful stewards of what God has given.
