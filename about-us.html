Vision & Mission

Vision StatementThe Leading Christian School

To be the leading Christian School of its kind in the country, bridging faith in Almighty God with the heart and passion for academic excellence and Christian values.

MissionFuture Leaders of the Nation

To develop Christian students to become future leaders of our nation with love and fear of the Lord.

What We Do

Our Mission Statement

Four Commitments to Every Child

  • To lead every child to Jesus Christ (John 3:3)
  • To teach every child the Word of God (2 Timothy 2:15)
  • To teach every child to truly fear God (Romans 5:13)
  • To teach every child the way of holy living to worship and glorify God (Colossians 2:11; 2 Cor. 6:17)

Our Philosophy

We provide a sound academic education integrated with a Christian view of God and the world. If the Bible is true, then what is truly true must be grounded in the Word of God. Faith and reason are not in conflict — they are inseparable in the HDICS classroom.
